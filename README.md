 Around The World Electronic Store – Power BI Project

[Project Overview]
This project analyzes global sales performance across multiple international branches of an electronic retail store.  
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights using Power BI and DAX.

The goal is to evaluate revenue performance, discount impact, product contribution, and operational efficiency.

---

[Dashboard Pages]

(Page 1) – Global Sales Overview
Executive summary of business performance including:
- Total Revenue
- Revenue After Discount
- Total Orders
- Total Units Sold
- Average Order Value
- Discount Percentage
- Revenue by Branch
- Sales by Salesperson
- Sales Trend Over Time

---

(Page 2) – Product Performance Analysis
Deep dive into product-level insights:
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Sub Category
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Average Unit Price
- Total Discount Amount
- Product & Sub Category Count

---

[Key Business Insights]
- Identifies high-performing branches
- Highlights top revenue-generating products
- Measures impact of discounts on profitability
- Analyzes category-level contribution
